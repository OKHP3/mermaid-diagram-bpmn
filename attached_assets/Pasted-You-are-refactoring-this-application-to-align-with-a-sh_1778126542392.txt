Design system name:
OKH Forge UI System

- Teal/green structural accent
- Dark code/workbench panels
- Subtle blueprint/grid texture
- Serious toolbench utility
- Low-gloss, high-clarity interface
- Technical but human-authored
- More “productized prototype” than “generic generated dashboard”

Create a small reusable design layer.
